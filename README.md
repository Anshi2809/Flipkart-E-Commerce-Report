⏩Situation:

Flipkart’s multi-year sales data (2015–2021) lacked a unified analytical view to understand revenue growth, customer behavior, product performance, profitability trends, and target achievement—especially the sharp business shift observed in 2020.

⏩Task:

Design an end-to-end Power BI dashboard to deliver actionable insights across Orders, Products, Customers, Profit Forecasting, and Target Analysis, enabling data-driven decision-making for business and marketing stakeholders.

⏩Action:

✅Cleaned and modeled data using Power Query & DAX

✅Built interactive dashboards with slicers (Year, Month, Category, Rating, Age Group, Status)

✅Created KPIs for Revenue, Profit, Orders, Customers, Ratings

✅Used advanced visuals:

      - Waterfall (incremental profit analysis)

      - Forecasting (revenue trends)

      - Donut & treemap (customer & product mix)

      - Target vs Actual gauges

      - Drill-through & tooltip pages

      - Analyzed 2020 spike using trend, variance, and forecast visuals

⏩Result:

✅Identified 2020 as the highest growth year across revenue, profit, and orders

✅Revealed Adults as the dominant customer segment and high return-risk zones

✅Highlighted top-performing products & subcategories by profit

✅Enabled target gap analysis, showing revenue and profit shortfalls

✅Delivered a scalable, executive-ready dashboard improving insight accessibility and business storytelling
